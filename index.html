Methodology

How Vlad's Authentic Scan Works

Our multi-layered detection pipeline analyzes 14 distinct signals across metadata, frequency domains, and neural fingerprints.

1

Upload Your File

Drag & drop or browse any image, video, or PDF. Files up to 50MB are supported. Your file is loaded securely into memory.

2

3

Confidence Score

Each signal contributes to a weighted confidence score. You get a clear AI/Real verdict with full breakdown and downloadable report.

Detection Signals
What we analyze
🔬

GAN Artifact Detection

Generative Adversarial Networks leave distinctive checkerboard patterns in the frequency domain. We detect these at sub-pixel level.

📡

Noise Profile Analysis

Real cameras produce characteristic sensor noise. AI images have unnaturally uniform or synthetic noise distributions.

🗂️

EXIF Metadata Integrity

Authentic photos contain rich camera metadata. AI-generated images typically lack this or have inconsistent EXIF data.

🌊

Frequency Domain Scan

DCT and FFT analysis reveals AI-specific frequency artifacts that are invisible to the naked eye but detectable mathematically.

👁️

Facial Geometry Check

AI faces often have subtle geometric inconsistencies — asymmetric eyes, off-ratio ears, or impossible lighting angles.

🗜️

Compression Pattern

JPEG compression leaves distinctive block artifacts. AI images have unusual compression signatures that differ from camera originals.

Our Story

Built to protect truth in the age of AI.

Vlad's Authentic Scan was founded with one mission: to give anyone — from journalists to everyday users — the tools to verify whether media is real or AI-generated.

As AI-generated images and deepfakes become indistinguishable from real content, the need for fast, reliable, accessible detection has never been more urgent.

2023
Project started as a research initiative to study GAN detection methods, analyzing over 500,000 AI-generated images.
