About Us

Built to find the truth

Vlad's Authentic Scan was created because the internet is filling with AI-generated content, and people deserve a simple, free tool to know what's real.

🎯 Our Mission

We believe in digital transparency. As AI-generated images, videos, and documents become indistinguishable from real ones, the ability to verify content becomes a basic right. Vlad's Authentic Scan makes that verification accessible to everyone — for free.

🔬 Our Technology

Our detection engine analyzes noise fingerprints, GAN artifacts, diffusion model signatures, compression patterns, and metadata consistency. We combine multiple detection methods to give you a reliable confidence score rather than a simple binary guess.

🔒 Privacy First

Your files are analyzed in your browser and never uploaded to or stored on our servers. We don't collect, sell, or use your files for any purpose. What you scan stays private.

🌐 Community Driven

The community feed lets users share verified results (anonymously) to help each other identify common AI-generated content patterns circulating online. Together we build better detection coverage.
